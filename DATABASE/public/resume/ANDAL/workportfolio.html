About Ange

I'm a 19-year-old freshman studying at Gordon College, taking the BSEMC course to pursue my dream of becoming an animator. Before I began this journey or found inspiration, I felt lost and burned out. I didn't know how to rebuild myself—I was frustrated and hopeless. That changed when I read the manga Vagabond, a story about the legendary swordsman Musashi Miyamoto. I finished it in just 3–4 days, and during that time, I learned something powerful: true strength lies not in conquest, but in understanding. Thanks to that story, I finally found the inspiration to move forward—not just to dream, but to act. Nothing is impossible as long as you try.
